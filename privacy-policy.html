Information We Collect

1.1 Information provided by the user

We collect the information you provide directly when creating an account and using the App:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Password (securely encrypted)
  • Phone number (when applicable)
  • Pet-related information (such as name, age, breed, and history)

1.2 Automatically collected information

We may collect limited technical data necessary for the proper functioning of the App:

  • Device identifiers
  • Basic usage information (e.g., feature interactions)

The App does not collect location data and does not use third-party analytics tools currently.

2

How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Create and manage your user account
  • Enable the App's core functionalities
  • Store and organize your pets' information
  • Send notifications (such as reminders and important updates), when authorized by you
  • Improve user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations
3

Push Notifications

The App may send push notifications related to:

  • Pet care reminders
  • Important updates about the App

You may disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

4

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share your data only in the following cases:

  • With essential service providers (e.g., cloud hosting infrastructure)
  • When required by law or by competent authorities
5

Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, alteration, or disclosure.

Passwords are stored securely using encryption.

Data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct incomplete or outdated data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw your consent
